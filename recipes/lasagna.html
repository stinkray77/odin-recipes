Recipe for Lasagna

Description

Homemade Lasagna is a family favourite and a great way to feed a crowed

This lasagna recipe is relatively easy to make and has so much flavour

Ingredients

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat
  • Sauce
  • Spinach

Steps

  1. Boil Pasta
  2. Prepare Meat Sauce
  3. Combine cheese mixture
  4. Layer & bake
